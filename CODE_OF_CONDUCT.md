# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful and practical place for collaboration. Contributors and users are expected to communicate with patience, clarity, and good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds and skill levels.
- Keep feedback specific and technical.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when something goes wrong.
- Respect copyright, licenses, and platform rules.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic arguments.
- Requests or contributions focused on abuse, unauthorized access, or copyright infringement.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or serious violations may lead to a ban from the project community.
